Mission Statement:

“The Mission of Jeans Store is to sustain responsible commercial success as a global marketing company of branded apparel. We will conduct our business ethically and demonstrate leadership in satisfying our responsibilities to our communities and to society. Our work environment will be safe and productive and characterized by fair treatment, teamwork, open communications, personal accountability and opportunities for growth and development.”
